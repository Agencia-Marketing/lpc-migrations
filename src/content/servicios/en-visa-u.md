**LPC Services prepares your U Visa application.** This immigration status is designed for victims of certain crimes who have suffered substantial physical or mental abuse and have cooperated with law enforcement in the investigation or prosecution of the crime.

### What this service includes

- Police certification management (Form I-918B)
- Collection of medical reports and records
- Organization of evidence of harm suffered
- Personal statement preparation
- Complete Form I-918 packet assembly
- Certified translations when needed

### Who is this for?

This service is for **crime victims** who have suffered substantial physical or mental abuse and have been helpful or are likely to be helpful in the investigation or prosecution of criminal activity.
