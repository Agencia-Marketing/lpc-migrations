**LPC Services helps you prepare your affirmative asylum application.** We organize your personal statement, gather supporting evidence, and translate necessary documents so your file arrives complete to USCIS.

### What this service includes

- Drafting and structuring your personal asylum declaration
- Reviewing your story to identify key persecution points
- Collecting and organizing documentary evidence
- Certified translations of relevant documents
- Complete Form I-589 packet assembly
- Final consistency review before submission

### Who is this for?

This service is for individuals who **entered the United States legally** (with a visa, ESTA, or parole) and wish to apply for protection based on a well-founded fear of persecution in their home country.
